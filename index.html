About Akash Pharande

Akash Pharande is the Managing Director of Pharande Spaces, one of Maharashtra's leading real estate development companies known for creating large-scale integrated townships and modern residential communities. With a strong academic background that includes a Master's degree from IIM Bangalore and an MBA from Pune University, he combines business acumen, technical expertise, and strategic thinking to drive innovation in the real estate sector.

Over the years, Akash Pharande has played a pivotal role in transforming Pharande Spaces from a conventional real estate developer into a recognized name in township planning and execution. His commitment to quality, transparency, and customer-centric development has helped the company establish a strong reputation across Pune and other regions of Maharashtra.
